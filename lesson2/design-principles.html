Visual Hierarchy

According to Interaction-design.org " Visual hierarchy is the principle of arranging elements to show their order of importance. Designers structure visual characteristics—e.g., menu icons—so users can understand information easily. By laying out elements logically and strategically, designers influence users’ perceptions and guide them to desired actions."

I think that Amazon did an excellent job of following Visual Hierarchy.
