11 Things You Need to Know Before Traveling to Cozumel

Vacation in Cozumel, and odds are that you'll go home utterly relaxed and with memories of spectacular scenery and friendly people. A 34-mile-long by 11-mile-wide island off Mexico's Riviera Maya, Cozumel is quite unlike its neighbor. It's largely undeveloped, with expanses of jungle and untouched shoreline that are a rarity on the mainland. Travelers don't come for the partying, boisterous beach bikini volleyball games, or designer shopping. Instead, it's mostly about unplugging and enjoying the sun.
